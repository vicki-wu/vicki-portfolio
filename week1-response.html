Week 1 Reading Response

Something I found interesting about this presentation is the author's approach to web development as a journey of self-expression, rather than just a technical exercise. When I create websites, I tend to focus on the technical aspects such as creating fancy designs, rather than the content itself. After reading through the presentation, I noticed how simple the presentation was (how it was only made through HTML/CSS), yet I found it to be very impressive. I now agree with the author's perspective, particularly their emphasis on content over flashy features and the idea of websites being in "perpetual beta." This view encourages continuous learning and improvement, which is valuable in both web development and personal growth. The author's advice to focus on self-satisfaction rather than social media validation is also notable, as it promotes a healthier approach to creativity. This presentation reminds me of this website, which shows art only through a single div element with CSS and minimal HTML.
